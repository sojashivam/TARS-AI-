Frequently Asked Questions

       

Quick answers to common questions.

       
           

How secure are my documents?

           

All uploaded documents are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your data with third parties.

       
       
           

What file types do you support?

           

Currently, we support PDF files and common image formats (JPG, JPEG, PNG).

       
       
           

Do you offer an API?

           

Yes, we provide an API for developers to integrate our PDF and image analysis capabilities into their applications. Check our API documentation for more details.
